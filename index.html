Welcome!

This (lousy) minimalist website I made overnight is a showcase of my writings, featuring summaries I would love to transform into feature-length screenplays and hopefully, one day, into actual movies.

Spanning 4 to 20 pages, these summaries explore themes that I feel strongly connected to â€” time, love, loss, guilt, and the myriad facets of the human psyche. I attempt to explore genres such as science fiction, psychological drama, romance, and at times, horror. In these stories, I plunge into the depths of the human soul, trying to expose both its humanity and darkness.

In crafting these tales, I draw philosophical inspiration from the likes of Nietzsche, Kant, and Sartre, among others.
